About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Electronics & Telecommunication (ET) aims to impart quality education in the fields of computer science and electronics. The department focuses on fostering innovation, research, and holistic development in students.

Mission: To produce industry-ready professionals in the domains of CSE and ET.

Vision: To be recognized as a leader in education and research in CSE and ET, contributing to the advancement of technology.

Department Achievements

  • Ranked in the top 5 for research output in the region.
  • State-of-the-art laboratories in AI, ML, and Robotics.
  • National-level competitions won by students.
  • Collaborations with leading tech companies for research and placements.
