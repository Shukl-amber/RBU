Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world, known for their agility, playfulness, and affectionate nature. They are highly territorial animals and have a strong sense of smell and hearing. Cats are also known for their grooming habits, spending a significant amount of time cleaning themselves. With over 70 recognized breeds, cats come in a wide range of sizes, coat lengths, and colors, making them a diverse and fascinating species.
